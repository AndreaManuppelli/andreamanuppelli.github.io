Color Palette:

A deep, serene palette evoking a nocturnal landscape. Dominant shades of blue (#011b2a, #1b2a6a) anchor the design, while a warm yellow accent (#f0c204) adds energy. Crisp white text (#f5f5f5) ensures optimal readability against the dark backdrop

  • Primary: #011b2a

    The main color of the website.

  • Secondary: #1b2a6a

    The secondary color of the website.

  • tertiary color: #f0c204

    The tertiary color of the website. used for highlights

  • Text color: #f5f5f5

    The main text color of the website.
